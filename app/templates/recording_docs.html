Recording Instructions

Purpose

You are helping collect training data for a face authenticity detection system. Consistency matters.

Recording Rules

  • 45 seconds per recording, set automatically, don't worry
  • Front-facing webcam
  • No filters or beauty modes
  • Record as many videos as you can, that is, real and fake videos

REAL Recordings

  • You are physically present
  • One face only.
  • Vary the following, lighting, distance from webcam etc to get all sorts of videos for training
  • Blink normally
  • Small head movements are allowed

FAKE Recordings

  • Screen playback of a face
  • Printed photo
  • Deepfake playback
  • Get a live deepfake generator, for example https://deeplivecam.net/

Do NOT

  • Change lighting mid-recording
  • Wear hats or masks
  • Move the camera
